Deep Cleaning FAQ - The Villages

How long does a deep cleaning take in The Villages?

Deep cleaning typically takes 4-8 hours depending on home size and condition. Most Villages homes (1,200-2,500 sq ft) take 4-6 hours. We bring a team of 2-3 cleaners to work efficiently while maintaining our quality standards.

What's the difference between deep cleaning and standard cleaning?

Deep cleaning includes everything in standard cleaning PLUS: inside appliances, detailed bathroom tile/grout work, baseboards, light fixtures, ceiling fans, inside cabinets, window sills, and other areas typically not covered in regular maintenance cleaning. It's a comprehensive top-to-bottom service.

Do you offer special services for snowbirds?

Yes! Our "Snowbird Welcome Service" is perfect for seasonal residents returning to The Villages. We'll deep clean your home before your return, air it out, check for any issues, and can set up regular maintenance cleaning for your stay. Many Villages residents use this service annually.
