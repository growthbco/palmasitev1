Equestrian Cleaning FAQ - The Villages

Are your cleaning products safe for horses and other animals?

Yes, absolutely. We use only animal-safe, non-toxic cleaning products that are specifically chosen for their safety around horses, dogs, cats, and livestock. We understand the sensitivity of horses to chemicals and take extra care in product selection.

Do you have experience working around horses?

Our team includes staff with horse experience who understand farm operations, animal behavior, and safety protocols. We know how to work efficiently around horses and respect feeding schedules, turnout times, and training routines.

Can you clean while horses are in the barn?

We can work around horses when necessary, but prefer to coordinate cleaning with turnout schedules when possible for everyone's safety. We're flexible and will work with your farm's routine to minimize disruption to both horses and daily operations.

Do you serve properties near the World Equestrian Center?

Yes! We serve equestrian properties throughout The Villages area, including those near the World Equestrian Center. Many of our clients are involved in WEC events and we understand the higher standards expected in this prestigious equestrian community.

What makes equestrian property cleaning different from regular house cleaning?

Equestrian properties have unique challenges: mud rooms need frequent deep cleaning, hay and feed dust requires special attention, equipment areas need organization, and everything must be done with animal-safe products. We understand these specific needs and have specialized procedures for each area.
