Frequently Asked Questions - The Villages Standard Cleaning

How often should I schedule standard cleaning in The Villages?

Most Villages residents prefer bi-weekly standard cleaning, which maintains a consistently clean home without being overwhelming. Weekly service is popular for larger homes or those who entertain frequently, while monthly service works well for smaller homes or snowbirds.

Do you work with Villages gate access and golf cart paths?

Absolutely! Our team is very familiar with The Villages' unique layout, gate procedures, and golf cart accessibility. We coordinate with residents for smooth entry and work efficiently around the community's traffic patterns.

What's included in standard cleaning vs deep cleaning?

Standard cleaning focuses on maintaining cleanliness with regular tasks like dusting, vacuuming, bathroom cleaning, and kitchen maintenance. Deep cleaning is more intensive and includes inside appliances, baseboards, light fixtures, and detailed work. We recommend starting with a deep clean, then maintaining with standard service.

Are you licensed and insured in Florida?

Yes, Palma Cleaning Co is fully licensed and insured in Florida. We carry general liability and bonding insurance for your peace of mind. All our staff are background-checked and trained in our quality standards.

Do you offer services for snowbirds returning to The Villages?

Yes! We offer special welcome-back cleaning services for seasonal residents returning to The Villages. This includes a thorough cleaning to refresh your home after months away, plus we can set up your regular standard cleaning schedule for the season.
