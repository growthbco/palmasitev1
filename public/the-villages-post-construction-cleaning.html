Post-Construction FAQ - The Villages

What's included in post-construction cleaning?

Post-construction cleaning includes construction dust removal, window and fixture cleaning, floor cleaning and polishing, cabinet detailing, light fixture cleaning, debris removal, and complete sanitization. We make your project move-in ready.

Do you work with Villages contractors and builders?

Yes! We partner with many contractors and builders throughout The Villages. We offer contractor pricing, priority scheduling, and can coordinate with your project timeline. Many contractors use us for both rough and final cleaning phases.

How quickly can you complete construction cleanup?

Most Villages construction cleanup projects are completed within 1-2 days, depending on size and scope. We understand construction schedules are tight and can often accommodate rush jobs with advance notice. Same-day service is sometimes available.

What's the difference between rough clean and final clean?

Rough clean removes major debris and dust during construction, preparing for final trades. Final clean is the detailed cleaning after all work is complete - removing fine dust, cleaning all surfaces, and making the space move-in ready. Many projects need both phases.

Do you provide supplies and equipment for construction cleanup?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies, equipment, and tools. This includes industrial-grade vacuum equipment, specialized cleaning products, safety equipment, and disposal materials. Everything is included in our service pricing.
