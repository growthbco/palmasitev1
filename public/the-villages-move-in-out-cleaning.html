Move-in/Move-out FAQ - The Villages

How quickly can you clean a Villages home for move-in?

We offer same-day and next-day service for move-in cleaning when possible. Most Villages homes can be cleaned within 4-6 hours. We understand real estate timelines are tight and work to accommodate urgent situations.

Do you work with Villages real estate agents?

Yes! We partner with many real estate professionals in The Villages. We offer volume discounts, priority scheduling, and streamlined billing for agents and property managers. Contact us about our professional partnership program.

What's included in move-out cleaning?

Move-out cleaning includes everything in our deep cleaning service: inside appliances, cabinets, drawers, detailed bathroom work, baseboards, light fixtures, floors, windows, and removing all traces of occupancy. Perfect for getting deposits back or preparing for new owners.

Can you clean while movers are working?

We prefer to clean after movers are completely finished for the best results and safety. However, we can coordinate timing and work around moving schedules when necessary. Communication is key for a smooth transition.

Do you handle unique Villages features like golf cart garages?

Absolutely! We're familiar with all Villages home features including golf cart garages, lanais, screened porches, and outdoor living spaces. These areas are included in our comprehensive move-in/move-out service at no extra charge.
