Kitchen Cleaning Checklist

Daily Tasks (10 minutes)

  • □ Load/run dishwasher
  • □ Wipe down counters
  • □ Clean sink and faucet
  • □ Sweep floor
  • □ Put items back in place
  • □ Empty trash if full

Weekly Tasks (20 minutes)

  • □ Clean exterior of appliances
  • □ Wipe cabinet fronts
  • □ Clean backsplash
  • □ Mop floor thoroughly
  • □ Clean inside of microwave
  • □ Sanitize cutting boards

Monthly Deep Clean (45 minutes)

  • □ Clean inside of oven
  • □ Deep clean refrigerator
  • □ Scrub range hood
  • □ Clean inside cabinets
  • □ Degrease behind stove
  • □ Clean light fixtures

Pro Tip: In Ocala's humidity, clean your refrigerator coils every 3 months to maintain efficiency and prevent mold growth.

Bathroom Cleaning Checklist

Daily Tasks (5 minutes)

  • □ Squeegee shower after use
  • □ Hang up towels
  • □ Quick toilet wipe
  • □ Put items back in place
  • □ Run exhaust fan during/after shower

Weekly Tasks (25 minutes)

  • □ Scrub and disinfect toilet
  • □ Clean sink and faucet
  • □ Clean mirror and medicine cabinet
  • □ Scrub shower/tub
  • □ Sweep and mop floor
  • □ Replace towels and bath mat
  • □ Empty trash

Monthly Deep Clean (40 minutes)

  • □ Deep clean grout and tiles
  • □ Clean exhaust fan
  • □ Descale showerhead
  • □ Clean behind toilet
  • □ Organize medicine cabinet
  • □ Wash shower curtain/clean doors

Florida Tip: Use mold-prevention products in shower areas and keep exhaust fans running longer to combat humidity.

Living Room Cleaning Checklist

Daily Tasks (5 minutes)

  • □ Put items back in place
  • □ Fluff couch cushions
  • □ Fold and put away blankets
  • □ Empty trash baskets

Weekly Tasks (30 minutes)

  • □ Dust all surfaces
  • □ Vacuum upholstery
  • □ Vacuum or sweep floors
  • □ Clean TV screen
  • □ Dust electronics
  • □ Water plants
  • □ Straighten books/magazines

Monthly Deep Clean (45 minutes)

  • □ Clean ceiling fans
  • □ Dust light fixtures
  • □ Clean baseboards
  • □ Move furniture to vacuum under
  • □ Clean windows and window sills
  • □ Polish wood furniture

Bedroom Cleaning Checklist

Daily Tasks (5 minutes)

  • □ Make bed
  • □ Put clothes in hamper
  • □ Clear nightstand surfaces
  • □ Open curtains/blinds

Weekly Tasks (20 minutes)

  • □ Change bed sheets
  • □ Dust nightstands and dresser
  • □ Vacuum or sweep floor
  • □ Empty wastebaskets
  • □ Organize closet surface

Monthly Deep Clean (35 minutes)

  • □ Wash comforter/duvet
  • □ Clean ceiling fan
  • □ Dust baseboards
  • □ Vacuum under bed
  • □ Clean windows
  • □ Deep clean closet

Professional Cleaning Schedule

Make beds, do dishes, quick tidy up, manage clutter

Weekly (2-3 hours)

Bathrooms, kitchen deep clean, vacuum, dust, change sheets

Monthly (4-5 hours)

Deep clean appliances, baseboards, ceiling fans, windows

Seasonally

Deep decluttering, carpet cleaning, outdoor areas, HVAC

Professional Time-Saving Tips

Efficiency Hacks

  • Carry a cleaning caddy room to room
  • Set a timer for each room (prevents perfectionism)
  • Clean as you go throughout the day
  • Use microfiber cloths for streak-free cleaning
  • Start loads of laundry before cleaning

Family Teamwork

  • Assign age-appropriate tasks to kids
  • Create a family cleaning playlist
  • Use the "10-minute tidy" rule before bed
  • Implement "one touch" rule for items
  • Make it fun with games and rewards

Ocala Climate Tips

  • Clean early morning to avoid heat
  • Use humidity control in bathrooms
  • Clean pollen weekly during peak season
  • Address mold/mildew immediately
  • Protect furniture from UV damage
