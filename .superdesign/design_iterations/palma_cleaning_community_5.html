Our Story

Born & Raised in Central Florida

Hi there! We're the Martinez family, and we've called Central Florida home our entire lives. We started Palma Cleaning Co. in 2020 because we saw how hard it was for busy Ocala families to keep up with everything — work, kids, activities, and still maintain a clean, healthy home.

As parents of two young kids ourselves, we understand the struggle. That's why we created a cleaning service that truly gets it — flexible scheduling, family-safe products, and the personal touch that only comes from neighbors who genuinely care about your family.
