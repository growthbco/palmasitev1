Quick Emergency Cleaning Tips

When unexpected messes happen, these professional tips can save the day

Wine Spill Emergency

Act Fast: Blot (don't rub) immediately with clean cloth. Pour salt on red wine to absorb. For white wine, blot and rinse with cold water.

Professional tip: Club soda works better than water for red wine spills.

Pet Accident Cleanup

Step 1: Remove solid waste, blot liquid. Step 2: Use enzyme cleaner to eliminate odors. Step 3: Rinse with clean water and blot dry.

Never use ammonia-based cleaners - they can make the problem worse.

30-Minute Guest Prep

Priority areas: Entry, guest bathroom, main living space. Focus on surfaces guests will see and touch most.

Professional tip: A clean bathroom makes the biggest impression on guests.

Water Damage Response

Immediate action: Stop water source, remove standing water, increase air circulation. Document damage for insurance.

In Ocala's humidity, quick action prevents mold growth within 24-48 hours.
